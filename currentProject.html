Current Projects

Intelligent Power Management and Trading System (SEMTS)

The Smart Home Energy Management and Trading System (SEMTS) is a revolutionary application designed to improve home energy efficiency. It enables users to monitor and control household appliances and power production units in real time, optimising energy use and promoting energy self-sufficiency. Through intelligent analytics and personalized recommendations, SEMTS aims to reduce energy waste while providing a platform that allows users to sell or feed excess power back to the grid for efficient energy distribution. This project represents not only technological innovation, but also a commitment to environmental protection and sustainable development.
